## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Navigate to Registration Page**
   - Go to the Sahatak homepage
   - Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**

2. **Fill in Your Information**
   - Enter your **Full Name**
   - Enter your **Email Address**
   - Enter your **Phone Number**
   - Enter your **Age**
   - Select your **Gender**
   - Create a strong **Password**
   - Select **"Patient"** as your account type

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Click **"Register"** button
   - You'll receive a verification email

4. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link sent to your email
   - Or go to **"Verify Email"** page from your dashboard

### Logging In

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Click **"Login"** on the homepage

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Enter your **Email** or **Phone Number**
   - Enter your **Password**
   - Click **"Login"**

3. **Access Your Dashboard**
   - You'll be redirected to your patient dashboard

## Booking Appointments

### How to Book an Appointment

1. **Navigate to Appointments**
   - From your **Dashboard**, click **"Book New Appointment"** button
   - OR click **"Appointments"** in the sidebar, then **"Book Appointment"**

2. **Select a Doctor**
   - Browse the list of available doctors
   - **Filter by Specialty** (optional):
     - Click the specialty dropdown
     - Select the specialty you need (Cardiology, Dermatology, Pediatrics, etc.)
   - View doctor information:
     - Name and specialty
     - Experience level
     - Consultation fee
     - Availability status

3. **Check Doctor's Availability**
   - Click on a doctor to see their available time slots
   - Select a **Date**
   - Select an available **Time Slot**

4. **Add Appointment Details**
   - Enter your **Symptoms** or **Reason for Visit**
   - Add any **Additional Notes** for the doctor

5. **Confirm Booking**
   - Review your appointment details
   - Click **"Book Appointment"** or **"Confirm"**
   - You'll receive a confirmation message
   - The appointment will appear in your appointments list

## Managing Appointments

### Viewing Your Appointments

1. **Go to Appointments Page**
   - Click **"Appointments"** in the sidebar
   - Or click **"View Appointments"** from dashboard

2. **Browse Your Appointments**
   - See all your appointments listed with:
     - Doctor name
     - Date and time
     - Status (Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled)
     - Appointment type
   - Use **pagination** to navigate multiple pages

3. **View Appointment Details**
   - Click on any appointment to see full details
   - View symptoms, notes, and appointment information

### Rescheduling an Appointment

1. **Open Appointment Details**
   - Go to **Appointments** page
   - Click on the appointment you want to reschedule

2. **Click "Reschedule"**
   - Look for the **"Reschedule"** button
   - Click it

3. **Select New Time**
   - Choose a new **Date**
   - Choose a new **Time Slot** from available times

4. **Confirm Reschedule**
   - Click **"Confirm"** or **"Reschedule"**
   - Your appointment will be updated

### Cancelling an Appointment

1. **Open Appointment Details**
   - Go to **Appointments** page
   - Click on the appointment you want to cancel

2. **Click "Cancel Appointment"**
   - Look for the **"Cancel"** or **"Cancel Appointment"** button
   - Click it

3. **Confirm Cancellation**
   - Confirm that you want to cancel
   - The appointment status will change to "Cancelled"

## Video Consultations

### Joining a Video Consultation

1. **When It's Appointment Time**
   - Go to your **Appointments** page
   - Find your scheduled appointment

2. **Join the Consultation**
   - Click **"Join Video Consultation"** button
   - The button becomes available close to your appointment time

3. **Video Consultation Screen**
   - Your video feed will appear
   - Wait for the doctor to join
   - You'll see the doctor's video when they join

4. **During the Consultation**
   - Speak with your doctor
   - Discuss your symptoms and health concerns
   - The doctor may take notes or prescribe medications

5. **Ending the Consultation**
   - The doctor will typically end the consultation
   - Or you can click **"Leave"** or **"End Call"** if needed
   - Click **"Complete Appointment"** when finished

### Checking Video Status

- You can check if the doctor is ready by viewing the appointment status
- The status will show: Waiting, In Progress, or Completed

## Medical Records

### Accessing Your Medical Records

1. **Navigate to Medical Records**
   - Click **"Medical Records"** in the sidebar
   - Or click **"View Medical Records"** from dashboard

2. **View Your Health Information**
   - See your complete medical history
   - View vital signs (heart rate, blood pressure, temperature, etc.)
   - Access diagnoses from doctors
   - View test results and reports

### Viewing Medical History

1. **Go to Medical History Page**
   - From Medical Records, click **"Medical History"**

2. **Review Your Information**
   - **Blood Type**
   - **Allergies**
   - **Chronic Conditions**
   - **Current Medications**
   - **Surgical History**
   - **Emergency Contact Information**

### Completing Your Medical History

1. **Check Completion Status**
   - Go to **Medical History** page
   - If incomplete, you'll see a prompt to complete it

2. **Fill in Missing Information**
   - Click **"Complete Medical History"** or **"Update"**
   - Enter all required information:
     - Blood type
     - Known allergies
     - Chronic conditions
     - Current medications
     - Past surgeries
     - Family medical history

3. **Save Your Information**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Submit"**
   - Your medical history is now complete

### Updating Your Medical History

1. **Go to Medical History Page**
   - Click **"Medical Records"** → **"Medical History"**

2. **Click "Update" or "Edit"**
   - Make changes to your information
   - Add new allergies, medications, or conditions

3. **Save Changes**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Update"**

### Viewing Vital Signs History

1. **Access Vital Signs**
   - Go to **Medical Records**
   - Look for **"Vital Signs"** section

2. **Review Your Vitals**
   - See history of recorded vital signs:
     - Heart rate
     - Blood pressure
     - Temperature
     - Weight
     - Oxygen saturation
   - View dates when vitals were recorded
   - Track changes over time

## Communicating with Doctors

### Accessing Communication Hub

1. **Navigate to Messages**
   - Click **"Messages"** or **"Communication Hub"** in the sidebar
   - Or click **"Chat with Doctor"** from dashboard

### Starting a Conversation with a Doctor

1. **Click "New Conversation"**
   - Look for **"Start Conversation"** or **"New Message"** button
   - Click it

2. **Select Doctor**
   - Choose the doctor you want to message
   - Typically doctors you've had appointments with

3. **Write Your Message**
   - Type your message in the text box
   - Keep it clear and concise

4. **Send Message**
   - Click **"Send"** button
   - Your message will be delivered to the doctor

### Viewing Conversations

1. **Go to Messages Page**
   - Click **"Messages"** in sidebar

2. **See Your Conversations**
   - List of all conversations with doctors
   - See most recent messages
   - Unread conversations are highlighted

3. **Open a Conversation**
   - Click on a conversation to view full message history

### Sending Messages in a Conversation

1. **Open the Conversation**
   - Click on the conversation from your list

2. **Type Your Message**
   - Use the message input box at the bottom
   - Type your question or message

3. **Send Message**
   - Click **"Send"** button
   - Or press **Enter** on your keyboard

### Using Quick Templates

1. **In a Conversation**
   - Look for **"Quick Templates"** or template icon

2. **Select a Template**
   - Choose from pre-written messages:
     - "I need to reschedule my appointment"
     - "I have a question about my prescription"
     - "I'm experiencing side effects"
     - etc.

3. **Send or Edit**
   - Send the template as-is
   - Or edit it before sending

### Marking Messages as Read

- Messages are automatically marked as read when you open them
- Unread count appears on the Messages menu

### Searching Messages

1. **In Messages Page**
   - Look for **"Search"** box

2. **Enter Search Terms**
   - Type keywords to search for
   - Press **Enter** or click **Search**

3. **View Results**
   - See matching messages
   - Click to open the conversation

## Profile & Settings

   - Select **"Profile"** or **"View Profile"**
   - Or click **"Profile"** in the sidebar

2. **See Your Information**
   - Personal details
   - Contact information
   - Medical information
   - Account status

### Editing Your Profile

1. **Open Profile Page**
   - Click **"Profile"** in sidebar

2. **Click "Edit Profile"**
   - Look for **"Edit"** or **"Update Profile"** button

3. **Update Information**
   - Change your **Name**
   - Update **Phone Number**
   - Update **Age**
   - Change **Gender**
   - Update **Blood Type**
   - Modify **Emergency Contact** information

4. **Save Changes**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Update"**
   - Your profile will be updated

### Changing Your Password

1. **Go to Settings**
   - Click **"Settings"** in sidebar

2. **Find Password Section**
   - Look for **"Change Password"** or **"Security"**

3. **Enter Password Information**
   - Enter your **Current Password**
   - Enter your **New Password**
   - **Confirm New Password**

4. **Save New Password**
   - Click **"Change Password"** or **"Update"**
   - You'll need to log in again with the new password

### Changing Language Preference

1. **In Settings or Top Menu**
   - Click the **language selector** (usually top right)
   - Or go to **Settings** → **"Language"**

2. **Select Language**
   - Choose **Arabic** or **English**

3. **Apply Changes**
   - The interface will update immediately
   - Or click **"Save"** if in Settings

### Updating Patient Preferences

1. **Go to Settings**
   - Click **"Settings"** in sidebar

2. **Patient Preferences**
   - Look for **"Preferences"** section

3. **Update Your Preferences**
   - Notification preferences
   - Communication preferences
   - Appointment reminders

4. **Save Preferences**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Update"**

### Deactivating Your Account

1. **Go to Settings or Profile**
   - Click **"Settings"** in sidebar

2. **Find Account Section**
   - Look for **"Account"** or **"Deactivate Account"**

3. **Click "Deactivate Account"**
   - Read the warning message
   - Confirm deactivation

4. **Confirm Action**
   - Your account will be deactivated
   - You can reactivate by contacting support

## Tips for Using Sahatak

1. **Keep Your Medical History Updated**
   - Regularly update your medications and conditions
   - This helps doctors provide better care

2. **Be Prepared for Video Consultations**
   - Test your camera and microphone before appointments
   - Be in a quiet, private location
   - Have a list of questions ready

3. **Communicate Clearly**
   - Be specific about symptoms
   - Mention when symptoms started
   - Note any changes in your condition

4. **Check Messages Regularly**
   - Doctors may send important follow-up messages
   - Respond promptly to doctor inquiries

5. **Book Appointments in Advance**
   - Schedule appointments early for better availability
   - Reschedule if needed rather than missing appointments

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?
- Check your email/phone and password
- Use **"Forgot Password"** to reset
- Verify your email if you haven't already

### Can't Book an Appointment?
- Make sure you've completed your medical history
- Check if the doctor has available time slots
- Try a different doctor or date

### Video Not Working?
- Check your internet connection
- Allow browser to access camera and microphone
- Refresh the page and try again
- Contact support if issues persist

### Email Verification Issues?
- Check spam/junk folder
- Click **"Resend Verification Email"** from dashboard
- Contact support if you don't receive it
