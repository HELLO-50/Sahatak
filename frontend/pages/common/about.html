Service Details

Book appointments for video or in-person consultations with doctors. For video consultations, simply select your preferred doctor and time, then join the secure video call from your dashboard. Our system uses AI to assist with symptom checking and provides a chat system for sharing information and asking questions during the session.

Schedule laboratory tests directly through Sahatak. Choose your test, select a lab, and book a convenient time. Results are securely delivered to your medical records and can be discussed with your doctor via video or chat.

Book radiology and imaging appointments, such as X-rays or MRIs, through our platform. Receive digital results and consult with specialists using our integrated video and chat system.

Request prescription renewals online. Doctors review your request and, if approved, prescriptions are sent directly to your account. Use our chat system to clarify medication instructions or ask questions.

View and manage your medical records securely. Share records with doctors during consultations or via chat. AI tools help organize and summarize your health data for easier understanding.

Schedule vaccinations for yourself or family members. Receive reminders and access vaccination records. Use chat to ask questions about vaccine types and schedules.

Track and manage chronic conditions with regular check-ins, medication reminders, and AI-powered health insights. Consult with specialists via video and chat for ongoing support.

Access mental health professionals for counseling and therapy sessions via secure video calls. Use the chat system for ongoing support and resources tailored to your needs.

Browse curated health tips, articles, and resources. AI recommends content based on your health profile and interests. Discuss articles with doctors or other users via chat.

Get round-the-clock medical support through chat or video. AI-powered triage helps direct your queries to the right professionals for timely advice.

Order prescribed medications for home delivery. Track your orders and receive notifications. Use chat to resolve any delivery issues or ask about your medications.

Manage health profiles for your family members. Book appointments, track vaccinations, and consult with doctors for each member. Use chat for family-specific health advice.

Get help with health insurance claims and coverage questions. Our chat system connects you with insurance experts for guidance and support.
