## Getting Started

### Creating Your Doctor Account

1. **Navigate to Registration Page**
   - Go to the Sahatak homepage
   - Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**

2. **Select Doctor Account Type**
   - Choose **"Doctor"** as your account type

3. **Fill in Your Information**
   - Enter your **Full Name**
   - Enter your **Email Address**
   - Enter your **Phone Number**
   - Enter your **Medical License Number**
   - Select your **Specialty** (Cardiology, Dermatology, Pediatrics, etc.)
   - Enter your **Years of Experience**
   - Create a strong **Password**

4. **Submit Registration**
   - Click **"Register"** button
   - You'll receive a verification email

5. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link sent to your email

### Logging In

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Click **"Login"** on the homepage

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Enter your **Email** or **Phone Number**
   - Enter your **Password**
   - Click **"Login"**

3. **Access Your Dashboard**
   - You'll be redirected to your doctor dashboard

## Account Verification

### Understanding Verification Status

**Important**: Your account must be verified by an administrator before you can:
- Accept patient appointments
- Access patient communication tools
- Review patient medical records
- Manage your availability schedule

### Verification Process

1. **After Registration**
   - Your account is created as **"Unverified"**
   - You'll see a **"Verification Required"** banner on your dashboard

2. **Verification Badge**
   - Look for the **badge** on your dashboard:
     - **"Unverified"** (red/yellow badge) - Awaiting admin approval
     - **"Verified"** (green badge) - Admin has verified your account

3. **Restricted Features (While Unverified)**
   - The following quick actions will show a **lock icon** and "verification required" overlay:
     - **Patient Communication**
     - **Review Records**
     - **Manage Availability**
   - You can still:
     - Complete your profile
     - View dashboard
     - Access settings

4. **After Verification**
   - Once admin verifies your account:
     - Badge changes to **"Verified"**
     - All features become accessible
     - You can start accepting appointments
     - Locks are removed from quick actions

5. **What to Do While Waiting**
   - **Complete your profile** (see [Completing Your Profile](#completing-your-profile))
   - Check your email for updates from admin
   - If waiting too long, contact support

## Managing Availability

### Setting Your Weekly Schedule

**Note**: This feature is locked until your account is verified by admin.

1. **Navigate to Availability Settings**
   - Click **"Set Availability"** from dashboard
   - Or click **"Manage Availability"** in sidebar

2. **Configure Weekly Schedule**
   - You'll see a schedule for each day of the week:
     - **Monday**
     - **Tuesday**
     - **Wednesday**
     - **Thursday**
     - **Friday**
     - **Saturday**
     - **Sunday**

3. **Enable/Disable Days**
   - For each day:
     - Check the box to **enable** the day
     - Uncheck to **disable** (day off)

4. **Set Working Hours**
   - For each enabled day:
     - Set **Start Time** (e.g., 9:00 AM)
     - Set **End Time** (e.g., 5:00 PM)
   - Use the time pickers or dropdowns

5. **Save Schedule**
   - Click **"Save Schedule"** or **"Update Availability"**
   - Your availability is now set

### Toggling Online/Offline Status

1. **On Your Dashboard**
   - Look for the **Availability Toggle** button
   - Shows current status: **"Online"** or **"Offline"**

2. **Toggle Status**
   - Click the toggle to switch:
     - **Online**: Patients can see you and book appointments
     - **Offline**: You're temporarily unavailable
   - Status updates immediately

3. **When to Use**
   - Toggle **Offline** during breaks or emergencies
   - Toggle **Online** when ready to accept appointments

## Video Consultations

### Starting a Video Consultation

1. **When Appointment Time Arrives**
   - Go to **Appointments** page
   - Find the scheduled appointment

2. **Click "Start Video Consultation"**
   - Look for **"Start Video"** button
   - Click to launch the video room

3. **Video Consultation Screen**
   - Your video feed appears
   - Wait for patient to join
   - Patient video appears when they join

### During the Consultation

1. **Communicate with Patient**
   - Discuss symptoms and health concerns
   - Ask relevant questions
   - Perform visual examination if needed

2. **Take Notes** (if available)
   - Use note-taking feature during call
   - Record key information

3. **Video Controls**
   - **Mute/Unmute** microphone
   - **Turn on/off** camera
   - **End Call** button

### Recording Medical Information During Consultation

- After or during consultation, you can:
  - Record vital signs (see [Recording Medical Information](#recording-medical-information))
  - Add diagnoses
  - Prescribe medications

### Ending a Video Consultation

1. **Click "End Call" or "End Consultation"**
   - Finish the video session

2. **Complete the Appointment**
   - Click **"Complete Appointment"** button
   - Add any final notes
   - Appointment status changes to "Completed"

### Joining an Ongoing Consultation

1. **If Patient Started First**
   - Go to **Appointments**
   - Find the appointment with "In Progress" status

2. **Click "Join Video Consultation"**
   - Enter the video room
   - Continue the consultation

### Video Consultation Monitoring

- **Heartbeat/Keep Alive**: System automatically sends heartbeat signals to maintain connection
- **Check Status**: You can check video consultation status at any time
- **Analytics**: System tracks video consultation analytics for quality monitoring

## Recording Medical Information

### Recording Patient Vital Signs

1. **After Searching for Patient**
   - Find the patient in EHR
   - Click to open patient record

2. **Click "Record Vital Signs"**
   - Look for **"Add Vital Signs"** or **"Record Vitals"** button

3. **Enter Vital Sign Data**
   - **Heart Rate** (beats per minute)
   - **Blood Pressure** (systolic/diastolic)
   - **Temperature** (°C or °F)
   - **Weight** (kg or lbs)
   - **Height** (cm or inches)
   - **Oxygen Saturation** (%)
   - **Respiratory Rate** (breaths per minute)

4. **Save Vital Signs**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Record"**
   - Vitals are added to patient's history

### Recording a Diagnosis

1. **Access Patient Record**
   - Search for and open patient's EHR

2. **Click "Add Diagnosis" or "Record Diagnosis"**
   - Look for diagnosis section

3. **Enter Diagnosis Information**
   - **Diagnosis Name/Description**: Enter the medical condition
   - **ICD Code** (if required): Enter diagnostic code
   - **Severity**: Select severity level
   - **Notes**: Add additional notes or observations
   - **Date**: Confirm date of diagnosis

4. **Save Diagnosis**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Record"**
   - Diagnosis is added to patient's medical history

### Viewing Patient's Vital Signs History

1. **In Patient Record**
   - Click **"Vital Signs"** tab or section

2. **Review History**
   - See all recorded vital signs
   - View dates and values
   - Track trends over time

### Viewing Patient's Diagnoses History

1. **In Patient Record**
   - Click **"Diagnoses"** or **"Diagnosis History"** tab

2. **Review History**
   - See all past diagnoses
   - View dates and prescribing doctors
   - Review notes and details

## Prescriptions Management

### Creating a New Prescription

1. **Navigate to Prescriptions**
   - Click **"Prescriptions"** in sidebar (if available)
   - Or access from patient record/appointment

2. **Click "Create Prescription" or "New Prescription"**
   - Look for create button

3. **Fill in Prescription Details**
   - **Select Patient**: Choose the patient (may be pre-selected from appointment)
   - **Medication Name**: Enter the drug name
   - **Dosage**: Specify dosage (e.g., "500mg")
   - **Frequency**: How often to take (e.g., "Twice daily", "Every 8 hours")
   - **Duration**: Length of prescription (e.g., "7 days", "2 weeks")
   - **Instructions**: Special instructions for patient (e.g., "Take with food", "Avoid alcohol")
   - **Quantity**: Total quantity to dispense

4. **Set Prescription Status**
   - **Pending**: Awaiting pharmacy fulfillment
   - **Active**: Currently valid and being used
   - **Completed**: Prescription period finished
   - **Cancelled**: No longer valid

5. **Save Prescription**
   - Click **"Create"** or **"Save"**
   - Patient will be notified
   - Prescription appears in patient's prescription list

### Viewing Prescriptions

1. **Navigate to Prescriptions**
   - Click **"Prescriptions"** in sidebar

2. **Browse Your Prescriptions**
   - See all prescriptions you've created
   - Filter by status:
     - Pending
     - Active
     - Completed
     - Cancelled
   - Use pagination for multiple pages

### Viewing Prescription Details

1. **Click on a Prescription**
   - From prescriptions list, click any prescription

2. **See Full Details**
   - Patient name
   - Medication details
   - Dosage and frequency
   - Duration and instructions
   - Prescription date
   - Status

### Updating a Prescription

1. **Open Prescription Details**
   - Click on the prescription to update

2. **Click "Edit" or "Update"**
   - Look for edit button

3. **Modify Information**
   - Change dosage, frequency, duration, or instructions
   - Update status if needed

4. **Save Changes**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Update"**

### Changing Prescription Status

1. **Open Prescription Details**
   - Click on the prescription

2. **Click "Change Status" or Status Dropdown**
   - Select new status:
     - **Pending**: Not yet filled
     - **Active**: In use
     - **Completed**: Finished
     - **Cancelled**: Void

3. **Confirm Status Change**
   - Click **"Update"** or **"Save"**

### Viewing Prescription Statistics

1. **In Prescriptions Section**
   - Click **"Statistics"** or **"Stats"**

2. **Review Stats**
   - Total prescriptions created
   - Active vs completed
   - Most prescribed medications
   - Trends over time

## Participation Mode

### Understanding Participation Modes

Sahatak offers two participation modes for doctors:

1. **Volunteer Mode**
   - Provide free consultations
   - No consultation fee charged to patients
   - Support community healthcare

2. **Paid Mode**
   - Set consultation fee
   - Patients pay for appointments
   - Professional paid service

### Viewing Your Current Participation Mode

1. **Go to Settings**
   - Click **"Settings"** in sidebar

2. **Find "Participation" Section**
   - Look for **"Doctor Participation"** or **"Participation Mode"**

3. **View Current Mode**
   - See if you're in **Volunteer** or **Paid** mode
   - See your current consultation fee (if in paid mode)

### Switching to Volunteer Mode

1. **In Settings → Participation**
   - Click **"Switch to Volunteer Mode"** button

2. **Confirm Switch**
   - Read information about volunteer mode
   - Confirm you want to switch

3. **Mode Updated**
   - Your consultation fee is set to 0
   - Patients can book for free

### Switching to Paid Mode

1. **In Settings → Participation**
   - Click **"Switch to Paid Mode"** button

2. **Set Consultation Fee**
   - Enter your **consultation fee** amount
   - Set currency (if applicable)

3. **Confirm Switch**
   - Review fee amount
   - Confirm switch to paid mode

4. **Mode Updated**
   - Patients will see and pay your consultation fee
   - Fee shown when booking appointments

### Updating Participation Settings

1. **In Settings → Participation**
   - Look for **"Update Settings"** or **"Edit"**

2. **Modify Settings**
   - Change consultation fee (in paid mode)
   - Update other participation preferences

3. **Save Changes**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Update"**
